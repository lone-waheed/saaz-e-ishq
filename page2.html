When Names Whisper…

“Unhone naam nahi poocha tha… yaad poocha tha.”

There’s a reason why silence deepens when a name is remembered. Because it never was just a name.

Sometimes it’s a story. A moment. A day that never ended. A message that was never sent.

This one… was a name that arrived like a breeze — and stayed like an echo.

“Aik baar phir uss naam ne khamoshi ko likhna sikha diya…”
