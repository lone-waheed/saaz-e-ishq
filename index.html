Every silence has a name…

Some doors don’t need keys —
They open with memories.

A name once saved in a contact list… and then saved in a heart.

He asked her to save it — a name that meant nothing to the world,
but everything in their silent world.

She smiled. Maybe even teased him. But she saved it anyway.
And from that day, even her silence felt warmer.

Remember it?
It wasn’t Waheed. It wasn’t a poet. It wasn’t a prince.
It was the one who appeared as a joke… but stayed as a prayer.


“The moment you remembered… the silence bloomed like spring.”
